Chicago, on Lake Michigan in Illinois, is among the largest cities in the U.S. Famed for its bold architecture, it has a skyline punctuated by skyscrapers

It is a great place to visit with family, friends, and couples. There are many enjoyable attractions in the Chicago area that are inexpensive or even free. Some free attractions can be found within the parks of downtown Chicago. Another inexpensive attraction in downtown Chicago is Navy Pier. Some other inexpensive attractions in Chicago are the different museums and exhibits scattered around the downtown area.

Visit Chicago at any time of the year. There will alway be something interesting to do and enjoy. Chicago experiences the full range of weather, with blustery winters and sweltering summers. The best times to visit for milder weather is in late Spring, early summer, and early fall.

So you might think why Chicago?

Twenty-six miles of uninterrupted lakefront, accentuated by 15 miles of sandy beaches and a 18.5-mile stretch of Lakefront Trail. Our 77 globally diverse neighborhoods. Approximately 60 museums. Multi-sensory art, architecture, and green space. And many more....
